8/17/2022

It's been 6 months! Where have you been?

Okay, I've been gone for quite a while. Not that anyone would notice. But I've been working on things in the background.

First off, I began work on a project for this website.
It would have been a site where you could upload your Minecraft player save file and it would show you everything that's saved within.
But in the end, after putting quite a bit of work into it, I decided that it was dumb and deleted everything.

Then I've kind of just sat idle doing nothing really.
Well, I did... something, but it's still too soon to talk about that. Maybe it will never even see the light of day.
Who knows.

But for a long time now, I wanted to do something. Not just for myself.
But I never quite got the motivation to do so.
I hope this will change very, very soon.

I have ideas, visions of projects that could be great if I would just find the motivation to complete them.
Many of those are sitting on my hard drive, uncompleted and abandoned.
I made them in times where I was less experienced and today I would have done so many things different.
That makes it very difficult to come back to them.

I also feel like some projects need an entire overhaul.
Especially the quiz site here is something I'm not happy with.
But can I find the motivation to make that happen?
How would I even go about it?
Should I just... remove it entirely?

No. I can't just delete things I don't like.
Either I change it until it's good or it stays bad.
But Xeraction, didn't you delete your latest project because you didn't like it?
No no, you didn't understand it right. It wasn't finished, so I can delete it at any time.

What am I doing.
I bind myself to my own rules and am afraid of breaking them.
What a misery.

See you, probably.
